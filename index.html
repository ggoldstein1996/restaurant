King's Garden

Located in historic Chestnut Hill, King's Garden has been serving authentic Chinese cuisine to the Philadelphia region for more than 20 years. Our creative and passionite chef's select the menu based on seasonability and quality of ingredients, and are proud to serve only the freshest of produce available. Locally-grown vegetables and Lancaster beef are just some of the sustainable ingredients that go into our mouth-watering dishes! Service is of the utmost importance to our customers, and our waiting staff is highly trained and eager to make your dining experience the best it can be.
